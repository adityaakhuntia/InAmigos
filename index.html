Who We Are

A Legacy of
Care & Compassion

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Govind Shukla, the InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit driving sustainable, inclusive growth. We take bold, actionable steps toward the most pressing challenges of our time.
